February 18, 2018

Day of Service

Every year, the team gets to spend a Saturday practice volunteering with a local organization on our Annual Day of Service. The team was excited to visit the Humane Society of Dickson County for the third time. While there, we cleaned out the kennels, helped feed the dogs, and did some laundry. The highlight of the day was getting to give all the puppies baths. As you can see in the pictures, most of the team joined in this activity! We enjoyed spending the day out in Dickson, thank you to Dickson County Humane Society for having us!

Winter Recruitment, Be part of the Crew!>

Interested in joining Vanderbilt Rowing? Join us at one of our practices on 1/15 or 1/16 at 5 in Rec room West 1B. You can meet the team and learn how to use an erg! See you there! November 15th, 2017

Head of Hooch

On November 4th and 5th, the team competed at the Head of the Hooch in Chattanooga, TN. This regatta had close to 200 teams in attendance and each race had more than 20 boats with some races having close to 60 boats. The competition was fast and it was a great opportunity for the team to see where we stand against other schools as we head into winter training. We had strong results for many boats with several falling just short of medaling. This was the last head race of the fall season for the team.

October 23, 2017

Secret City

On Saturday, October 21st, the team competed in the Secret City Head Race in Oak Ridge, TN. We posted very strong results and showed off some of our development over the past few weeks, especially among the novice team, who competed in two fours. Schools that attended included the University of Tennessee, UTC, and more! The results for all boats as well as some pictures from this weekend are below. Special thanks to our novice team members Aaron Wong, Sandra Shaw, and Claire Kim for the pictures.
